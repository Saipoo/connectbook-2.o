❓ TROUBLESHOOTING:

Problem: "No internships found" or "No hackathons found"
Fix: Check backend console - did seed complete? Try manual seed.

Problem: Frontend shows errors
Fix: Press Ctrl+Shift+R (hard refresh browser)

Problem: Seed says "already exists" but frontend shows nothing
Fix: Database has data but might be corrupted. Clear MongoDB and restart.
